== IMPROVEMENT LOOP ==

This is the key: you must iterate. A typical progression:

  Round 1: Random moves           → avg ~1,000, 0% win rate
  Round 2: Simple heuristic       → avg ~5,000, 0% win rate
  Round 3: Better heuristic       → avg ~10,000, 5% win rate
  Round 4: Expectimax search      → avg ~20,000, 30% win rate
  Round 5: Tuned expectimax       → avg ~30,000, 60% win rate
  Round 6: Advanced techniques    → avg ~40,000+, 90%+ win rate

After each evaluation, analyze:
  - What tiles are you reaching? (check tile distribution)
  - Where are games ending? (board state at loss)
  - Is your AI fast enough? (must be <100ms per move on average)

== STRATEGY HINTS ==

Level 1 - Greedy heuristics:
  - Prefer down/left moves (keep big tiles in corner)
  - Count empty cells (more = better)
  - Check monotonicity (rows/columns in order)

Level 2 - Search:
  - Expectimax: evaluate future board states with random tile spawns
  - Depth 2-3 is usually sufficient for good play

Level 3 - Advanced:
  - N-tuple networks with TD learning
  - Monte Carlo tree search
  - Transposition tables for caching

- Focus on ITERATION — don't try to build the perfect AI in one shot
